About Us

OLD CLASHERS is a top-tier Free Fire esports team from Bangladesh, known for unmatched synergy, fast-paced tactics, and fearless execution in battle. Our team consists of elite players with unique specialties, working together to dominate every match. From close-combat assaults to precision sniping, we cover all bases.


Founded by passionate players, the team quickly rose in ranks through local and national tournaments. We aim to represent the South Asian esports community on global stages with pride and professionalism.
